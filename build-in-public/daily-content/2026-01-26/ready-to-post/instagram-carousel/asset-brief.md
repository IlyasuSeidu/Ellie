# Instagram Carousel Asset Brief

- Audience: people likely to save, share, or forward a compact lesson
- Objective: turn one day into a saveable story or framework
- Native format: 7-slide narrative or breakdown
- Target length: 7 slides
- Hook rule: Lead with a cover line that makes the swipe worth it.
- Structure: cover, real user tension, wrong assumption, what changed, proof, lesson, and question
- Cover style: bold lesson or problem statement
- CTA style: save-and-comment
- Overlay mode: light
- Angle mix: Wrong Assumption + Before/After Reveal

- Proof line: By the end of the day, setup had a clearer path, a one-question-at-a-time introduction, and a swipeable way to choose a roster pattern.
