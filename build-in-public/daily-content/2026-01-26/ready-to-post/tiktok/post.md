## Post

### Script

I rebuilt the roster picker because tired people should not have to think that hard just to start.

The setup was asking the right questions. It just felt like admin work. And when something feels like paperwork on the first screen, people start doubting everything after it.

So I reworked the flow. One question at a time. Swipeable cards for the roster pattern so you recognize your schedule instead of decoding it.

The welcome moment now feels like the start of something useful, not a form you have to survive.

You do not fix a hard decision by explaining it better. You fix it by changing how it feels to move through.
