# Hook Bank

Use these as opening lines for Reels, TikTok, Shorts, LinkedIn, X, or Threads. Every hook carries tension, proof, or a reaction trigger.

1. If choosing your roster feels like paperwork, people quit before the app earns trust.
2. I built the pattern picker three times before it felt right.
3. Shift patterns are boring. The interaction could not be.
4. The problem was not lack of information. It was lack of momentum.
5. A list can be accurate and still make people stop.
6. I stopped asking users to study shift jargon and started letting them move through it.
7. The onboarding win was not more screens. It was a smoother path between them.
8. If the gesture feels broken, the delight disappears instantly.
9. The fix was not more explanation. It was a better interaction.
10. The hardest onboarding choice should feel like progress, not admin.
11. What onboarding step in most apps still feels like paperwork to you?
12. People do not know the official name of their roster. They know what their weeks feel like.
