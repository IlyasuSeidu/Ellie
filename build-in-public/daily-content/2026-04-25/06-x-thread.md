# X / Threads Thread

1. If choosing your roster feels like paperwork, people quit before the app earns trust.

2. That was the real problem on day 2 of Ellie.

3. The morning tightened the path: onboarding navigation, a stronger introduction screen, and country selection.

4. The harder problem was shift pattern selection.

5. Most workers do not know the official name of their pattern first. They know what their weeks feel like.

6. So the fix was not more explanation. It was a better interaction.

7. I rebuilt the pattern step as swipeable cards so the hardest choice in setup felt lighter and easier to move through.

8. Product lesson: you do not make a boring decision better by explaining it harder. You make it better by changing how it feels.
