# Funnel Cuts

Use the same day of work to feed every stage of the funnel instead of publishing one recap and moving on.

## Awareness

- Platform: TikTok / Reels
- Asset: Pattern interrupt short
- Draft: Hook with "If 'Tomorrow: Night Shift' is wrong, the animation does not matter." then show the welcome screen.

## Consideration

- Platform: Instagram Carousel
- Asset: Trust sequencing breakdown
- Draft: Show the two jobs from day 1: the answer layer first, the visible layer second.

## Decision

- Platform: LinkedIn
- Asset: Founder judgment post
- Draft: Explain why the visible layer only works because of what came first underneath it.

## Activation

- Platform: Short-form video follow-up
- Asset: One practical product lesson
- Draft: Teach the principle that trust is structural, not cosmetic.

## Retention

- Platform: Stories / Threads
- Asset: Open loop + reply mining
- Draft: Ask what people secure first when the user cannot afford a wrong answer, then use the replies for tomorrow's angle.

## Advocacy

- Platform: LinkedIn / X
- Asset: Opinion post
- Draft: Turn the day into a sharp viewpoint: if the underneath truth is shaky, good design only delays the loss of trust.

## Publishing Order

1. Post the short video first while the tension is strongest.
2. Follow with the carousel for saves and shares.
3. Publish the LinkedIn post for the structural lesson.
4. Use replies to turn trust questions into the next chapter.
