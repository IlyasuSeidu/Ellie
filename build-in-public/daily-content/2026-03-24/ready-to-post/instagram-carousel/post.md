## Post

### Slide Copy

1. The translation was right. The trust was wrong.
2. In Ellie, long-language CTA text wrapped at decision moments and made the product feel less reliable.
3. Wrong assumption: "Readable copy" means the flow is clear.
4. Better approach: treat copy length, layout behavior, and motion as one product decision.
5. We shortened key CTA text across 11 locales and hardened onboarding/paywall layouts against long strings.
6. Result: cleaner first-minute flow, less hesitation, stronger trust where people decide to continue.
7. What "small" onboarding detail is actually a trust breaker?

### Caption
