# Funnel Cuts

Use the same day of work to feed every stage of the funnel instead of publishing one recap and moving on.

## Awareness

- Platform: TikTok / Reels
- Asset: Pattern interrupt short
- Draft: Hook with "If choosing your roster feels like paperwork, people quit before the app earns trust." then show the swipeable cards immediately.

## Consideration

- Platform: Instagram Carousel
- Asset: Wrong-assumption breakdown
- Draft: Show that the issue was not missing information. It was the weight of the interaction.

## Decision

- Platform: LinkedIn
- Asset: Founder judgment post
- Draft: Explain why onboarding friction is often about admin-feel, not lack of instructions.

## Activation

- Platform: Short-form video follow-up
- Asset: One practical lesson from the build
- Draft: Teach the principle that the hardest onboarding choice should feel like momentum, not form-filling.

## Retention

- Platform: Stories / Threads
- Asset: Open loop + reply mining
- Draft: Ask which onboarding step still feels like paperwork, then use the replies to shape the next chapter.

## Advocacy

- Platform: LinkedIn / X
- Asset: Opinion post
- Draft: Turn the day into a sharper viewpoint: a technically correct flow can still lose people if it feels like admin.

## Publishing Order

1. Post the short video first while the contrast between list and swipe interaction is freshest.
2. Follow with the carousel for saves and shares.
3. Publish the LinkedIn post for the broader product lesson.
4. Use replies to identify which onboarding friction to tackle in the next post.
