# Hook Bank

Use these as opening lines for Reels, TikTok, Shorts, LinkedIn, X, or Threads. Every hook carries tension, proof, or a reaction trigger.

1. **Confession**: I thought this was a small cleanup. It turned into a trust problem.
2. **Specific Pain**: The fastest way to lose someone is to make the reliability layer and the invisible foundation feel uncertain in the first minute.
3. **Contrarian**: Most "polish" work is not visual. It is meaning arriving faster.
4. **Before/After**: Before today, the reliability layer still made people pause. After today, the path is much harder to miss.
5. **User Lens**: Nothing was broken. People were still hesitating. That is the problem I care about.
6. **Result First**: The reliability layer now reads faster. The story is why it did not before.
7. **Builder Lesson**: If someone has to stop and interpret your screen, the screen is not done.
8. **Emotional Angle**: I want the app to feel like it understands you before it asks anything from you.
9. **Founder Opinion**: I trust products more when they feel clear quickly than when they look impressive slowly.
10. **Open Loop**: The question I care about now is whether the hesitation is actually gone.
11. **Community Prompt**: What is one tiny moment in an app that instantly makes you trust it less?
12. **Callout**: If the reliability layer sounds like a tiny change, that is usually where the real story lives.
