# YouTube Shorts Asset Brief

- Audience: searchers, builders, and viewers willing to stay for a clearer explanation
- Objective: hold attention long enough to teach one real product lesson
- Native format: slightly deeper vertical explainer
- Target length: 30-60 seconds
- Hook rule: Hook immediately, then explain with one clean progression.
- Structure: hook, context, decision, proof, lesson, and next question
- Cover style: clear lesson or wrong-assumption line
- CTA style: question-led
- Overlay mode: light-builder
- Angle mix: Stakes / Consequence + Wrong Assumption

## Story Fit

- Chapter: when onboarding stopped feeling like paperwork

- Proof line: By the end of the day, setup had a clearer path, a one-question-at-a-time introduction, and a swipeable way to choose a roster pattern.
